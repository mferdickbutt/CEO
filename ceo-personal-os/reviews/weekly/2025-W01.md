## How to Use Weekly Reviews

1. **Do this every week** — Friday afternoon or Sunday evening
2. **Review your daily check-ins** from the past week before starting
3. **Look for patterns** — is the same friction showing up? Is energy consistently low?
4. **Use this to adjust** — don't just observe, act on what you learn
5. **Reference this in quarterly reviews** to see trends over 12 weeks

---

## What This Reveals Over Time

After 12-16 weeks of weekly reviews, you'll see:
- **What actually drives results** vs. what just feels busy
- **Recurring energy drains** that need to be eliminated or delegated
- **Strategic insights** that compound into a mental model of how your business works
- **Whether your priorities are consistent** or if you're constantly shifting focus

**Weekly reviews are where tactics turn into strategy.**
